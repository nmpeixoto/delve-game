↑↓←→Move — d-pad, Arrow Keys, or W A S D
SwipeMove on mobile — swipe the map in any direction
BASH / BActivate Bash ability (see Combat tab)
STAIRS / . >Descend when standing on stairs >
SHOP / TEnter merchant when standing next to $
BAG / IOpen inventory to use or equip carried items
? / HOpen this help screen at any time
BumpWalk into an adjacent enemy to attack them automatically
TapTap or click a visible enemy to attack from up to 2 tiles away
⚡ BASHDeals double damage to the nearest visible enemy within 2 tiles. 5-turn cooldown. Use on tough enemies or when low on HP
DEFEach point of DEF reduces incoming damage by 1. Stack armor to survive deeper floors
XPKilling enemies grants XP. Level up to gain +1 ATK, +1 DEF, and +8 Max HP
TipEnemies you cannot see still wander. They pursue you once spotted
† ⚔ ♦Weapons — increases ATK. Auto-equipped if better than current
◈Armor — increases DEF. Auto-equipped if better than current
!Potions — restores HP instantly on pickup
RarityCommon · Rare · Legendary — higher rarity means stronger stats
BAGItems not auto-equipped go into your bag. Open BAG to manually equip a weaker item or use a saved potion
DropEnemies have a 20% chance to drop an item on death
$The merchant appears on most floors. Walk next to the $ tile and tap SHOP
💰 GoldEarned by killing enemies. Shown in the top-right HUD
BUYPurchase weapons, armor, potions, and permanent stat upgrades. Red price = can't afford yet
SELLSell items from your bag or equipped gear for 50% of their value. Useful for clearing space and funding better upgrades
UpgradesAvailable from floor 2: permanent boosts to ATK, DEF, or Max HP. Worth saving gold for
